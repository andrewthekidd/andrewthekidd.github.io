About us

Rex Vulpes Coffee Company began with a simple mission: to craft bold, smooth, and uncompromising coffee for those who demand excellence in every cup. What started as a passion for roasting small batches of high-quality beans quickly grew into a full-scale operation, with expertly sourced coffee roasted to perfection. With a commitment to precision and flavor, Rex Vulpes built a reputation for delivering rich, complex brews that fuel sharp minds and strong spirits.

Now, the journey has expanded beyond roasting—Rex Vulpes Coffee Company proudly welcomes customers to experience the craft firsthand at its newly opened café. More than just a coffee shop, the space embodies the brand’s dedication to quality and atmosphere, offering a place where coffee lovers can savor the same expertly roasted beans in a setting designed for conversation, creativity, and connection. Whether you’re looking for a quiet moment with a meticulously brewed espresso or a bold pour-over to jumpstart your day, the Rex Vulpes café is the perfect destination for those who appreciate coffee at its finest.
